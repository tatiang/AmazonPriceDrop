Amazon Price Drop Finder does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. The extension operates entirely locally in the user’s browser and only reads visible price and product text on Amazon cart and “Saved for later” pages in order to calculate percentage price drops and highlight qualifying items. No data is sent to external servers. No analytics, tracking, advertising, or profiling is performed. User preferences (such as percentage threshold and auto-highlight setting) are stored locally using Chrome’s extension storage. If you have questions, contact: tatiangreenleaf@gmail.com
